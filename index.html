About Me

Welcome to my webpage! My name is Tom Pocius. I am a full stack developer student with Northwestern University Coding Bootcamp looking to jump careers out of the medical field and into programming.

I recieved my bachelors degree from Elmhurst College with an Exercise Science degree and my doctorate in Physical Therapy at Governors State University. I have been working full time as an acute physiacl therapist at U Chicago Medicine at Ingalls Memorial Hospital since 2014 as an acute specialist. I specialize in communicating with medical professionals and facilitating mobility for critically sick patients, as well as, working with hospital administration for patient throughput during patient length of stay.

Currently I am at Northwestern Coding Bootcamp learning how to become a full stack developer. As of now I am proficient with html and css.
